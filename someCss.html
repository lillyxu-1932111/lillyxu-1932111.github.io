The boba from DTF is surprisingly good :D


As the Din Tai Fung website says, "Boba Milk Tea: Deliciously sweet and fun to drink. Our premium black tea is imported from Taiwan, freshly brewed in-house, and hand-shaken to order. Add our signature honey boba to this classic Taiwanese drink for a satisfying chew with every sip. We also offer a Green Milk Tea." It is vegetarian and gluten-free.
